# Methane and Refrigerants: Cutting the Non-CO₂ Climate Forcers

> **Type:** article
>
> **Date:** 2026-08-15
>
> **Scope:** Low-temperature methane conversion, methane pyrolysis, fluorine-free refrigerants, and solid-state refrigerants.
>
> **Description:** Methane and HFC refrigerants are the most important non-CO₂ climate forcers. Materials discovery for conversion, pyrolysis, and replacement is limited by the same defect/bulk asymmetry Lupine corrects.
>
> **Audience:** Catalysis researchers, chemical engineers, climate policymakers, investors
>
> **Status:** Draft

<p class="lead">Methane's 20-year warming potential is roughly eighty times that of CO₂. Hydrofluorocarbon refrigerants could add half a degree Celsius by century's end. Both problems are materials problems — and both sit in the same error geometry.</p>
