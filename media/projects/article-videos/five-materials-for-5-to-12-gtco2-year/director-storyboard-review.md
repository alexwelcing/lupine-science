## Decision rationale

The storyboard is approved for HyperFrames composition build. It gives the 92.376-second film a clear argument rather than a five-item list: a conditional climate-materials hook expands into five tangible mechanisms, collapses at “one shared failure,” peaks on the 100 meV → approximately 50× consequence, and resolves through correction, verification, and bounded refusal. Every approved narration sentence has a contiguous timed visual state, and the final claim remains a path to scale rather than a claim of delivered abatement.

The visual direction is aligned with Lupine’s proof-first system. The plan explicitly rejects stock crystals, generic AI networks, baked-text article images, unsupported numbers, and decorative motion. The motion ambition is sufficient for a designed short film: worlds transform causally into one another, mechanisms remain continuous across handoffs, charts reveal their evidence in sequence, and the shared-failure/refusal beats receive distinct visual authority.
