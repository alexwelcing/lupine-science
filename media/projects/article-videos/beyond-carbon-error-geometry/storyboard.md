## Direction

- **Thesis:** The shared failure is not “AI for climate” in general; it is a measurable geometry of model error concentrated in under-coordinated environments, and correction plus formal boundaries can improve which candidates deserve experiments.
- **Rhythm:** sweep–fracture–explain–invert–accelerate–focus–prove–expand–resolve. The seven-domain hook moves quickly, the atomic mechanism slows for comprehension, the ranking inversion snaps priorities into view, the stakes accelerate as a radial census, the field and proof beats become precise, and the close resolves into a short laboratory queue.
- **Emotional journey:** Start with curiosity at unlikely connections, turn that curiosity into discomfort when the shared blind spot is exposed, make the damage concrete through false experimental priorities, restore confidence through an inspectable measured field and bounded proof, then close with disciplined platform ambition rather than universal-validation hype.
- **Evidence peak:** Beat 06 — a scoped blind-test trace (`r = 0.906`, `n = 36`) passes through the correction pipeline and ends at explicit `SUPPORTED / OUT OF DOMAIN / SYNTHESIS-DEPENDENT` proof boundaries.
- **Visual doctrine:** Warm editorial scientific instrument, not dark AI spectacle. The recurring motif is a coordination contour that begins as a fracture, becomes a softening curve, distorts rankings, reconnects the planetary-stakes spokes, and finally becomes the measured error field and verified lab route.
- **Transition plan:** Six handoffs: four 12-frame indigo-line wipes (66.7%), one 15-frame field-line reveal into the measured-field mechanism, and one 21-frame paper fade into the canonical outro. No pre-transition exits; each outgoing trace becomes incoming evidence structure.
