# From Predicted Crystal to Commercial Cell — Narration Script

## Performance direction

Calm, precise, moderate pace. Treat the opening as a chain of negations that lands on a single problem. Let the numbers in the second beat arrive cleanly. Build the partner-chain beat with forward momentum. End on the word "trust" as the scarce resource.

## Beat sheet

### Beat 1 — Hook · 0:00–0:17

**Narration:** A predicted crystal is not a material. A synthesized powder is not a cell. A cell is not a fleet, a filter, a fertilizer plant, or a solar farm. Between a computational screen and a commercial product stands a chain of handoffs — and most predictions never survive it.

**Visual cue:** A wide crystal funnel narrows through synthesis, coating, cell fabrication, and module integration into one commercial cell. Title resolves: **PREDICTED CRYSTAL → COMMERCIAL CELL**.

### Beat 2 — The bottleneck · 0:17–0:33

**Narration:** Millions of predicted crystals shrink to a few hundred independently synthesized. The conventional answer is a bigger generator. But volume without trust only amplifies the same handoff failure. What is missing is a correction and verification layer that makes predictions trustworthy before they reach the lab.

**Visual cue:** A prediction counter collapses from **MILLIONS** to a small pile of verified powders; an indigo warning band marks the **TRUST GAP**.

### Beat 3 — A partner chain · 0:33–0:50

**Narration:** Consider cobalt-free lithium-manganese-rich cathodes. A corrected ranking sifts more than a million compositions, then passes the best candidates through synthesis, atomic-layer coating, pouch-cell fabrication, and finally into an automaker's prismatic cell line. Each handoff carries a bounded error budget, or it breaks.

**Visual cue:** Five linked stations — **RANK**, **SYNTHESIZE**, **COAT**, **FABRICATE**, **INTEGRATE** — pass a single candidate forward; broken links glow when the error budget is exceeded.

### Beat 4 — The correction · 0:50–1:08

**Narration:** The correction measures error as a field over local coordination, anchored to measured observables and pinned to zero in bulk. Analytic forces apply it at runtime, so simulations stay fast. Across 36 blind model-material pairs, corrected surface energies correlate at zero point nine zero six, with zero fitted parameters.

**Visual cue:** A smooth coordination-error field curve anchored by three amber knots and pinned to zero at bulk; a pipeline flows **uMLIP → CORRECTION → ANALYTIC FORCES** with the metric **r = 0.906 · n = 36**.

### Beat 5 — Verification as a filter · 1:08–1:24

**Narration:** The verification layer separates supported predictions from unsupported ones with machine-checked proofs. It can flag impossibility, not just uncertainty, before synthesis starts. Experimental budgets are finite; false positives are expensive. The layer makes sure the work goes to candidates that actually deserve it.

**Visual cue:** A proof ribbon stamps claims **SUPPORTED**, **BOUNDED**, or **UNSUPPORTED**; a rejected metastable framework exits the synthesis queue before any lab time is spent.

### Beat 6 — Platform thesis · 1:24–1:41

**Narration:** The same logic reaches solid-state electrolytes, direct-air-capture frameworks, ammonia catalysts, and lead-free perovskites. Different markets, different partners, same geometry of wrongness. In every chain, the scarce resource is not prediction volume. It is the trust that turns a predicted crystal into a commercial cell.

**Visual cue:** Four climate-material motifs orbit a shared coordination-defect geometry, then collapse into the closing thesis: **TRUST THE HANDOFF**.
