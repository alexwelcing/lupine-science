# From Predicted Crystal to Commercial Cell — Storyboard

This video tells materials engineers and climate-tech investors that prediction volume is no longer the limiting resource: a material reaches a commercial cell only when every partner can trust the physics and the evidence inherited from the previous handoff.

The 28 narration sentences below match `audio/narration.txt` and the 102.288-second final narration master verbatim. Timing boundaries come from the master waveform’s sentence pauses; scene handoffs live inside those pauses rather than extending the timeline.

## Direction

- **Thesis:** Lupine turns an unbounded prediction into a partner-ready candidate by correcting risky local physics, attaching machine-checked claims, and carrying a bounded error budget through the chain.
- **Rhythm:** contrast–collapse–BUILD–shock–repair–expand–resolve. Beats 1–2 move briskly from seductive abundance to the trust gap; Beat 3 deliberately accumulates six handoffs; Beat 4 is the quantitative evidence peak; Beat 5 repairs the mechanism; Beat 6 fans outward; Beat 7 compresses the whole argument into one continuous line.
- **Emotional journey:** Begin with dry, almost playful category corrections; let the viewer feel the long gap between a crystal and a truck; replace scale awe with sober distrust; peak on the 60% → 5,000× causal miss; close with disciplined confidence that one well-bounded candidate is worth more than another million predictions.
- **Evidence peak:** Beat 04 — a raw uMLIP can underestimate a lithium migration barrier by **60%**, moving predicted room-temperature conductivity by roughly **5,000×**.
- **Visual doctrine:** One persistent “candidate baton” passes between worlds. It is never a stock 3D crystal: use native lattice vectors, energy contours, process rails, and evidence marks. Existing article JPEGs are concept references only; rebuild production evidence as editable vector/data scenes without baked text.
- **Transition plan:** Four of six handoffs use the 12-frame indigo-line wipe (66.7%); the error consequence → Lupine repair turn earns one 15-frame field-line reveal; the final scene resolves through the 21-frame paper fade. The outgoing evidence trace becomes an axis, rail, contour, or certificate edge in the incoming world.
- **Audio notes:** Use the 102.288s Ana master at its current -16 LUFS integrated loudness. Bed stays at least 12 dB below narration. Sparse evidence-linked SFX only: three material-state locks, funnel collapse, six handoff clicks/cracks, barrier relay + accelerated ion ticks, analytic-force alignment, five route locks, one final continuous-line chime. Preserve the recorded pauses after sentence 5, 10, 13, 17, 21, and 25.
- **Caption plan:** Captions occupy x 192–1728 / y 828–954. All decisive charts, route labels, proof badges, and product silhouettes stay above y 810.
