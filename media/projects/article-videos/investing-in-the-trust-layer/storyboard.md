## Direction

- **Thesis:** Lupine is investable infrastructure because it measures model error, corrects it in the tools laboratories already use, and formally refuses claims the evidence cannot support.
- **Rhythm:** rush–freeze–plunge–assemble–build–reject–compound–resolve. The opening races through abundance before freezing on belief; the validation collapse creates the problem; the pipeline establishes Lupine’s position; the three-pillar build broadens confidence; the Lean rejection is the evidence peak; the flywheel names both moat and risk; the final condensation lands the investment case.
- **Emotional journey:** Begin with exhilaration and incredulity, turn abundance into unease, replace unease with inspectable confidence, peak on proof correcting its own claim, then close with sober ambition rather than hype.
- **Evidence peak:** Beat 05 — Lean rejects a statistically filtered claim and changes `27 / 36` to `26 / 36 VERIFIED`, proving that the trust layer can say no to itself.
- **Visual doctrine:** Warm editorial instrument, not dark AI spectacle. Reuse the funnel, measured-field, runtime-overlay, risk-map, and trust-loop concepts from the article visual storyboard, but rebuild them in the shared paper / ink / indigo frame system. Use the approved reusable title-card, data-chart, lower-third, and outro compositions where their contracts fit.
- **Transition plan:** Six handoffs: four 12-frame indigo-line wipes (66.7%), one 15-frame field-line reveal into the three-pillar mechanism, and one 21-frame paper fade into the outro. Every evidence trace becomes a rule, axis, contour, or route in the next scene; no separate pre-transition exits.
- **Audio notes:** Fast, warm, conversational delivery at 165–180 WPM. Leave micro-pauses after “believing the result,” “zero point two percent,” “say no,” and “part of the moat.” Bed: restrained paper-and-mechanism pulse at least 12 dB below narration. SFX remain evidentiary: candidate rush, funnel collapse, proof seal, three mechanical locks, theorem refusal, flywheel ratchet, final route lock.
- **Caption plan:** Reserve x 192–1728 / y 828–954. Keep all decisive charts, labels, theorem states, and gauges above y 810.

**Timing authority:** The visual descriptions below preserve the approved seven-beat concept.
