## Direction

- **Thesis:** The story is not that all methane mitigation waits for materials. Existing non-material interventions can act now; the film focuses on difficult residual streams and next-generation cooling where materials discovery remains a bottleneck. Their hardest screens meet at surfaces, radicals, transition states, and phase boundaries where raw universal models can soften barriers and invert rankings.
- **Rhythm:** measured hook–quick dual reveal–SLOW microscope–mechanical fork–accelerating sieve–SLOW proof–quiet deadline. The shared error mechanism is the first comprehension hold; the separate validation is the evidence peak; the close resolves rather than celebrates.
- **Emotional journey:** Begin with agency, reveal two large levers, narrow from policy scale to atomic failure, make the failure physical in methane and refrigerants, restore confidence through measured correction and honest boundaries, then return to the clock with disciplined urgency.

A single indigo evidence trace recurs as the warming arc, split-screen connector, coordination axis, reaction route, filter rail, correction spline, and final clock hand. No disaster montage, generic AI network, glossy molecule render, or universal-validation badge.
