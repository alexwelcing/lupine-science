## Executive decision

The storyboard is creatively approved in principle but not approved for animation lock. Its seven-beat arc is unusually clear for a technically dense subject, the recurring indigo evidence trace gives the two climate levers one coherent visual grammar, and the motion plan is causal rather than decorative. Brand alignment and motion ambition pass.

The blocker is source synchronization. The storyboard is explicitly conformed to a 133.704-second narration master that the narration director review rejects in five places. It therefore cannot be the timing authority for production, captions, or proof labels. One proof label also remains unresolved: Beat 6 still says the exact source-supported parameter wording is pending. These are lock blockers, not reasons to redesign the concept.

## What passes

### Narrative clarity

- The arc—near-term leverage → correctly scoped materials bottleneck → shared low-coordination failure → methane trap → refrigerant sieve → bounded correction proof → policy-clock close—is cumulative and easy to follow.
- Beat 2 preserves agency by keeping leak repair and capture visible instead of implying that all methane mitigation waits for new materials.
- Beat 3 converts an abstract model error into the consequence that matters: a softened barrier and false experimental winner.
- Beats 4 and 5 keep methane and cooling chemically distinct while showing precisely where their hardest screening steps meet.
- Beat 6 is the correct evidence peak: mechanism first, domain gate second, separate blind validation third.
- Beat 7 resolves on disciplined urgency and experimental prioritization rather than a universal-performance claim.

### Brand alignment

- Warm paper, ink, indigo, Newsreader, IBM Plex Mono, canonical-mark handling, and restrained semantic accents follow the Lupine system.
- Evidence precedes conclusions throughout: axes before curves, correction anchors before trajectory changes, and points before the correlation stamp.
- The plan visibly separates deployable non-material methane action, bulk refrigerant thermophysics, application prospectus, machine-checked bounds, and separate blind validation.
- `MAY ENABLE · VERIFY`, `PRIORITIZE FOR VERIFICATION`, `SEPARATE BLIND VALIDATION`, and `SUPPORTED ≠ VALIDATED EVERYWHERE` embody “evidence before claim.”
- The design avoids disaster imagery, generic AI networks, stock molecule spectacle, baked-text evidence, and universal-validation badges.
- Type floors, title-safe bounds, caption reserve, sources, and the untouched final identity hold are specified.

### Motion ambition

- The recurring evidence trace evolves meaningfully as warming arc, split divider, coordination axis, reaction route, filter rail, correction spline, fit line, and clock hand.
- Motion carries mechanism: neighbors disappear, barriers sag, rankings invert, methanol is pulled into over-oxidation, filter domains separate, measured anchors constrain a field, and the corrected trajectory bends only after the field exists.
- Each scene enters in causal phases rather than behaving like a card carousel.
- The transition family is coherent without becoming repetitive: four line wipes establish continuity, the field-line reveal marks the proof turn, and the paper fade quiets the close.
- The proof scatter populating in six waves before the fit and correlation stamp is an appropriately ambitious evidence reveal.
