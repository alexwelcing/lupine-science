## Direction

- **Thesis:** The water and air targets are different devices, but their decisive binding and barrier predictions occupy the same low-coordination risk zone; Lupine’s value is a measured, bounded correction layer, not a claim of completed validation across every application.
- **Rhythm:** sober hook–focus–sag–quick triptych–quick triptych–SLOW proof–quiet resolve. Human stakes register before the atomic mechanism narrows the frame; water and air move tactilely; the evidence peak deliberately slows; the close lands without a crescendo.
- **Emotional journey:** Begin with human scale rather than catastrophe, create unease as apparently fast screening becomes biased, build recognition across six concrete applications, restore confidence through scoped evidence, and finish with precise ambition.
- **Evidence peak:** Beat 06 — the measured field predicts a never-fitted fcc-metal surface-energy error across 36 model/material cells (`r = 0.906`, zero adjustable parameters), while the separate speed rail identifies the uMLIP-class advantage (`~100,000× vs DFT`).
- **Visual doctrine:** A warm editorial scientific instrument. A single coordination contour recurs as a waterline, neighbor-count axis, softened barrier, application route, measured field, and final trust thread. No generic atom/network imagery and no claim that water or air performance has already been corrected and validated.
- **Transition plan:** Six handoffs: four 12-frame indigo-line wipes (66.7%), one 15-frame field-line reveal into the proof beat, and one 21-frame paper fade into the outro. Transitions overlap adjacent worlds; no separate exit animation.
- **Audio notes:** Restrained ambient bed at least 12 dB below narration. Essential SFX only: two opening census ticks, neighbor-count clicks, barrier sag, three water route locks, three pollutant slips, three measurement anchors, scatter lock, speed rail, and one closing chime. Slow down on “fcc-metal blind test,” “surface-energy error,” and “zero adjustable parameters.”
- **Caption plan:** Reserve x 192–1728 / y 828–954. Keep all decisive marks, plots, proof labels, and source keys above y 810.
- **Timing authority:** Planned at 106.000s / 3,180 frames. Beat boundaries are F0000, F0420, F0870, F1290, F1770, F2220, F2820, and F3180. Re-conform to measured word timestamps after director-approved TTS; do not infer cue precision from this planning pass.
