<p class="lead">A predicted crystal is not a product. Between computation and climate impact lies a chain of partners — and Lupine is building the trust layer that holds the chain together.</p>

---

## The partnership-first thesis

Body: Lupine does not compete with structure generators, autonomous labs, or databases; it corrects and verifies their outputs, creating value at every layer of the discovery stack.

## Cobalt-free cathodes: from composition to OEM cell

Body: Tier-1 partners Manthiram/UT Austin/TexPower, Battery500, Forge Nano; tier-2 GM/Ultium, POSCO Future M, LG Energy Solution, Argonne APS. Path: screen → synthesize → coat → pouch cell → vehicle integration.

## Halide solid electrolytes: from Zr-Cl compositions to automotive cells

Body: Tier-1 partners Ceder/UC Berkeley & LBNL, University of Münster, Argonne, Solid Power; tier-2 Nazar/Waterloo, Factorial, Ion Storage, Battery500. Path: screen Li–Zr–Cl / Li–Fe–Cl → interface analytics → pouch cell → OEM validation.

## MOFs for DAC: from linker to gigafactory

Body: Tier-1 partners Yaghi/Long at UC Berkeley, Farha at Northwestern, BASF; tier-2 Svante, NETL, Climeworks, Atoco. Path: screen linkers → solvothermal synthesis → breakthrough testing → filter manufacturing → field deployment.

## Ammonia catalysts: from mediator to market

Body: Tier-1 partners DTU/Chorkendorff, Stanford/SUNCAT, Jupiter Ionics, ARPA-E REFUEL; tier-2 Caltech/Manthiram, Nitricity; tier-3 CF Industries, NREL. Path: non-Li mediator screen → flow-cell verification → MSA cell integration → distributed pilot.

## Lead-free perovskites: from absorber to tandem module

Body: Tier-1 partners NREL, University of Queensland/Wang Group, Tandem PV, Northwestern/Kanatzidis; tier-2 Oxford/Snaith, Swift Solar, Stanford/Karunadasa; tier-3 Oxford PV. Path: screen double perovskites → certified testing → tandem integration → module manufacturing.

## Cross-cutting architecture

Body: NREL as a four-target master CRADA candidate; UC Berkeley covering cathodes, halide SEs, and MOF DAC; ARPA-E portfolio strategy across REFUEL, IONICS, and OPEN.
