# Launch Video — Treatment

## Hook (0–5 s)
Open on an abstract, cinematic visualization of matter forming. The title appears:

> Unlocking the materials that build the future.

## Arc

1. **Problem (5–11 s)** — AI predicts matter, but its simulations are wrong in structured ways.
2. **Insight (11–16 s)** — Predictable failure is correctable failure.
3. **Proof (16–22 s)** — Trust you can check. Theorem-proved, open, and auditable.
4. **Product (22–28 s)** — Lupine Science: makeable materials, not fantasy frameworks.
5. **CTA (28–30 s)** — lupine.science

## Visual Style
Cinematic scientific abstraction: indigo and ochre over warm paper, slow organic motion, mathematical overlays, lupine flower motif. No live actors.

## Audio Mood
Hopeful ambient electronic: soft pads, gentle arpeggios, no vocals. Narration is calm, expressive, and authoritative.

## Narration Script

- Unlocking the materials that build the future.
- A.I. predicts matter, but its simulations are wrong in structured ways.
- Predictable failure is correctable failure.
- Trust you can check. Theorem-proved, open, and auditable.
