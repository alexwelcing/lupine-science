# Launch Video — Brief

## Goal
Announce Lupine Science to a technical and investor audience with a 30-second brand film that communicates the mission, the problem, and the proof.

## Audience
Materials scientists, AI researchers, deep-tech investors, and curious generalists.

## Format
- Type: 30-second launch video
- Resolution: 1280×720 (720p)
- Frame rate: 30 fps
- Platform: homepage hero, LinkedIn, X/Twitter, YouTube Shorts

## Must-include
- Lupine Science name and tagline
- The equation motif (`L(θ) = ∫ₘ ‖∇ᵧE‖² dμ(γ) + Ω(Ξ)`)
- The promise: trustworthy AI predictions of matter
- Evidence-before-claim tone
- CTA: `lupine.science`

## No-gos
- No direct GitHub link in the hero
- No unverified numbers
- No fantasy-framework language

## Approved CTA
Visit lupine.science
