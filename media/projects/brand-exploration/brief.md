# Brand exploration — brief

**Goal:** Use MiniMax image generation to explore the Lupine Science visual vocabulary at scale, then build a reviewable HTML gallery so the team can rate, compare, and iterate toward a locked creative direction.

**Audience:** Founder + Hermes creative/research agents evaluating pitch and site visuals.

**Outputs:**
1. A batch of on-brand stills exploring three motifs from the art direction.
2. An HTML gallery with scoring rubric and notes.
3. A short synthesis of what works and what to push next.

**Constraints:**
- Paper `#faf9f6` + indigo `#3d4db3` + ink `#16171d` palette.
- Editorial scientific minimalism; no flowers, people, text baked into images, neon, or generic AI tropes.
- Keep prompt DNA consistent so variants are comparable.
