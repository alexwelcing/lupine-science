# LUPI Launch Video — Treatment

## Hook (0–5 s)
A vast, dark molecular lattice fills the frame. Points of light ripple as the camera pulls back to reveal a million atoms arranged in a copper lattice.

> LUPI shows molecules.

## Arc

1. **Scale (5–11 s)** — Million-atom scenes, running in the browser.
2. **Clarity (11–16 s)** — Organic functional groups, OMol25 geometries, materials structures—inspectable, not just rendered.
3. **Evidence (16–22 s)** — See where models fail. Inspect the geometry of error.
4. **Product (22–28 s)** — LUPI. Browser-native evidence for atomistic science.
5. **CTA (28–30 s)** — open lupi.live

## Visual Style
Deep space black (#06080d) with electric indigo and soft white particles. Cinematic camera moves through lattices, molecules, and electron-density clouds. Clean, technical, no UI chrome until the end card.

## Audio Mood
Minimal, rhythmic electronic: sub-bass pulses, crystalline high-end, sparse percussion. No vocals. Sense of depth and precision.

## Narration Script

- LUPI shows molecules.
- Million-atom scenes, right in your browser.
- Functional groups. OMol25 geometries. Materials structures.
- See where models fail. Inspect the geometry of error.
- LUPI. Browser-native evidence for atomistic science.
- Open lupi dot live.
