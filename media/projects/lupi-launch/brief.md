# LUPI Launch Video — Brief

## Goal
Introduce LUPI as the browser-native molecular viewer for inspectable atomistic evidence. The film should make a technical audience want to open `lupi.live` and drop a structure into it.

## Audience
Materials scientists, computational chemists, MLIP builders, research software teams, and deep-tech investors.

## Format
- Type: 30-second launch video
- Resolution: 1280×720 (720p)
- Frame rate: 30 fps
- Platform: lupi.live homepage, LinkedIn, X/Twitter, YouTube Shorts

## Must-include
- LUPI name and URL (`lupi.live`)
- The idea of million-atom scenes in the browser
- The idea of evidence you can inspect, not just render
- Dark, scientific aesthetic matching LUPI (#06080d canvas)
- CTA: open lupi.live

## No-gos
- No claims about fake bonds or unsupported measured properties
- No legacy Atlas/retired viewer labels
- No pitch-deck language

## Approved CTA
Open lupi.live
