The hidden bottleneck

Generation is solved. Validation is the 0.2% problem.

GNoME predicted 2.2 million crystals; 380,000 were computed stable; only 736 had been independently synthesized by late 2023 — a 0.2% validation rate. The A-Lab autonomous synthesis facility reported 63% success, but subsequent review found many “novel” targets were already-known disordered phases. Faster synthesis without better verification does not close the gap.

Investor read

We are not short of predictions. We are short of validated predictions. Every false positive burns synthesis budget and calendar time the climate window does not have.
