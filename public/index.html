Step 1 of a real-world Replicator.

Simulation that can be trusted is the gate to everything after it: machines that design matter, then make it, then reprogram it. Nobody crosses that gate without an audit layer. That layer is what we are building — in public.

The science

Errors with a shape.

Compare many interatomic potentials against many materials and the errors don't scatter — they collapse onto a thin, ribbon-like manifold. That shape is the discovery: it means failure is predictable, and predictable failure can be corrected. The viewer, the library, the agents, and the formal layer all exist to measure, stress-test, and publish that one geometric object.
